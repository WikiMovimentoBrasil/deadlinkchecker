Dead link checker

Dead link checker makes a request to all the external links on a Wikipedia page and establishes whether the link is dead or not. It was developed in Outreachy27 by Alwoch Sophia in collaboration with Wiki Movimento Brasil as a request from the Lusophone technological wishlist.

The tool is inspired by deadlinkfinder, and credit goes to the original author for their contribution.
